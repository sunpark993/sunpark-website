Our Journey

Sunpark was established in 1995, beginning its operations with the trade of rich minerals, granite, and marble. Over the years, the company has grown into a trusted name in the global import and export industry.

Today, Sunpark proudly deals with a wide range of products including:

  • Minerals
  • Fertilisers
  • Spices, fruits, vegetables, dry fruits & nuts
  • Building materials including granite & marble

With exports reaching Far East Asia, Middle East, Europe, USA, Africa, Russia, and China, we are powered by a dedicated team of 16+ skilled professionals who ensure excellence in every shipment.
