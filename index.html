About Sunpark

Founded in 1995, Sunpark began its journey trading premium minerals, granite, and marble. Over the years, we have evolved into a trusted global import & export partner.

Our key export categories include:

  • Minerals
  • Fertilisers

With exports across Far East Asia, Middle East, Europe, USA, Africa, Russia, and China.
