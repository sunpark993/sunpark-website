About Sun park

Founded in 1995, Sun park began its journey trading premium minerals, granite, and marble. Over the years, we have evolved into a trusted global import & export partner.

Our key export categories include:

  • Fertilisers
  • Minerals
  • Building Materials (granite & marble)
  • Spices, Fresh Fruits & Vegetables, Dry Fruits & Nuts

With exports across Far East Asia, Middle East, Europe, USA, Africa, Russia, Australia, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Turkey, Oman, Dubai, Saudi, Qatar, Bahrain, Jordan, Singapore, Ghana, Tanzania, Cambodia, Canada, Brazil and China.
